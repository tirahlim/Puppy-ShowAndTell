My Favourite Teacup Dogs

What is Teacup Dogs?

Teacup Dogs are micro dogs that look like puppies forever. They are extremely popular and can be very costly.

Favourite Breeds

They are estimated about 10 breeds in the market. I will summarize on 3 breeds that I personnally adore alot.

  • Pomeranian
  • Pomsky
  • Pug

Pomeranian

Teacup Pomeranian is a small, active and adorable dog breed.

This breed tends to have a pretty sweet temperament.

Teacup poms usually weigh between 3lbs and 7lbs.

This breed is the most suitable for dog owners who live in apartments.

Pomsky

Teacup Pomsky is a crossbreed between Pomeranian and a Husky.

This breed tends to have an energetic and affectionate temperament so be prepared for lots of walks and play.

Teacup poms usually weigh up to 9lbs.

Pug

Teacup Pug is a small toy dog breed from China with a short mouth and smooth coat.

They are sturdy little dogs that pack in alot of personality. Pugs are typically sweet and good-natured, but they have some spunk in them too.

Teacup pug usually weigh up to 18lbs.
